SCHOOL NAME

Sattva Vikas School

Sattva Vikas School (SVS) located at On new 100 feet road, 2km off SG Highway from Pakwan intersection, Thaltej, Ahmedabad, Gujarat is one of the popular schools in India.

PRINCIPAL: MS JAYSHREE D. JOSHI MA'AM

SCHOOL AUDITORIUM

Before Covid, on Saturdays and special days, there were assemblies in the school auditorium and some students participate on the stage with the students of the whole school seeing them. They say speeches, dance, sing or, sometimes, even perform plays. Festivals like Janmashtami, Navratri, Independence Day and Christmas are celebrated here. Even in the pandemic, teachers shared videos in online meetings!




SCHOOL PLAYGROUND

Sattva Vikas also has a playground. Sometimes, the 1st period is Games period. We play games like Kho-Kho and Football here. Sports Day and its practice is held on this playground, and the Medals are also given there. The playground is near to the Canteen.




SCHOOL CANTEEN

Tasty food is given in the canteen! Sometimes, a birthday cake can be distributed to all! On Saturdays, it is not the usual roti-subji - something special is given. Pizzas are also given in the canteen!

CLASSES

The classes in Sattva Vikas School are:

Subjects: Main: Maths, English, EVS(until 5th, separated into Science and Social Science from 6th), Hindi, Gujarati, Other: Computer Science, General Knowledge, Sattva Samvardhan (how you should behave) and Environmental Education (from 6th)

Activities: Possible Online: Dance, Yoga, Art and Craft, Music, Physical Education, Other: Games, Library, Skating, etc.

The 4 Houses

There are four houses in our school, and on Mondays, you have to wear a house uniform instead of the normal one. The names of the houses are Agni (Orange), Jal (Blue), Bhumi (Green) and Vayu (White). Every house is like a team on Sports Day, you have to compete against another houses.
